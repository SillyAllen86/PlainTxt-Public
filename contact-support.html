Frequently Asked Questions

How do I sync documents across devices?

Plaintxt uses iCloud Drive for document synchronization. Make sure you're signed into the same Apple ID on all devices and have iCloud Drive enabled in your device settings.

Can I use Plaintxt without iCloud?

Yes! Plaintxt works perfectly without iCloud. Your documents will be stored locally on your device. iCloud sync is completely optional.

What file formats does Plaintxt support?

Plaintxt works with standard UTF-8 plain text files (.txt). This ensures maximum compatibility across different platforms and applications.

Is my data private and secure?

Absolutely. Plaintxt does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All documents remain on your device or in your personal iCloud Drive, which is controlled by Apple.

How do I report a bug?

Send an email to dtrhapps@gmail.com with details about the issue, including your device information and steps to reproduce the problem.
