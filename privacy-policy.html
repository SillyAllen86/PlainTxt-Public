Privacy Policy

Plaintxt - Simple, Focused Text Editor

Last Updated: December 2024

Plaintxt does not collect, use, or share any personal data. All documents are stored locally on your device and optionally synced via iCloud using Apple's secure infrastructure. No data is transmitted to third-party servers.

1. Information We Collect

We collect NO personal information. Plaintxt is designed with privacy as a core principle:

  • No user accounts or registration required
  • No analytics or usage tracking
  • No personal data collection
  • No third-party data sharing
  • No advertising or marketing data

2. Data Storage and Processing

Local Storage

All your documents are stored locally on your device:

  • Documents are saved as standard UTF-8 plain text files
  • No cloud storage unless you explicitly choose iCloud sync
  • All data remains under your complete control

iCloud Integration (Optional)

If you choose to enable iCloud sync:

  • Documents are synced using Apple's iCloud Drive service
  • All data is encrypted in transit and at rest by Apple
  • We have no access to your iCloud data
  • You can disable iCloud sync at any time

3. Third-Party Services

Plaintxt uses only Apple's built-in services:

  • iCloud Drive: For optional document synchronization (Apple's service)
  • SwiftUI & Foundation: Apple's native frameworks (no external dependencies)

We do not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services.

4. App Store Privacy Requirements

In compliance with App Store privacy requirements, Plaintxt:

  • No Data Collection: No personal data is collected or transmitted
  • No Tracking: No user tracking or analytics
  • No Third-Party Data Sharing: No data is shared with third parties
  • Minimal Permissions: Only requests necessary permissions for file access and iCloud sync

5. Data Security

Your data security is our priority:

  • All local data is protected by iOS/macOS security features
  • iCloud data is encrypted using Apple's end-to-end encryption
  • No data is transmitted to our servers (we don't have any)
  • App follows Apple's security best practices

6. Children's Privacy

Plaintxt is safe for users of all ages:

  • No personal data collection means no special considerations for children
  • App is rated 4+ (No Objectionable Content)
  • No user accounts or registration required
  • Parents can control iCloud sync through device settings

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.

Since we don't collect personal data, we cannot notify you directly of changes. Please check this page periodically for updates.

8. Your Rights

Since we don't collect personal data, there's no data to access, modify, or delete. However, you have complete control over:

  • Your local documents (stored on your device)
  • iCloud sync settings (controlled through device settings)
  • App deletion (removes all local data)
